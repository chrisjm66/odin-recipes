Chicken Parm

Description

Chicken Parm is a delicious Italian-American dish that has been enjoyed by all.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Panko Breadcrumbs
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Chicken
  • Salt
  • Basil
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare thinly-sliced chicken by patting it dry.
  2. Season flour with salt, basil, pepper, and any other desired spices.
  3. Cover the chicken in flour, then a beaten egg, then panko breadcrumbs.
  4. Add to a thin layer of olive oil and fry until golden brown and then flip.
  5. Let rest for 5 minutes and ensure the chicken is 165F internal temp.
  6. Taste for salt
  7. Add sauce, cheese, and broil or bake until it looks delicious. Enjoy!
