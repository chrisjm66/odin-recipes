Roasted Potatoes

Description

Roasted potatoes are a great side dish that go well with any meal.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Thyme
  • Potatoes

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water.
  2. In the meantime, dice potatoes to a bite-size pieces.
  3. Boil the potatoes until slightly under fork-tender. Strain.
  4. Toss potatoes in olive oil, garlic, salt, basil, rosemary, and thyme.
  5. Bake at 425F until golden brown.
  6. Enjoy!
