Vodka Sauce

Description

Vodka sauce is a delicious and easy to make sauce that goes great with pastas and other Italian dishes.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Vodka
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Thyme
  • Heavy Cream

Steps

  1. Heat up a pot, add olive oil, and saute one diced onion and as many cloves of garlic as your heart desires.
  2. When the onions are translucent, add the canned tomatoes and some vodka.
  3. Simmer for 15 minutes
  4. Once thickened, add basil, thyme, and heavy cream.
  5. Taste for salt
  6. Add to a pasta and enjoy!
